About us

Austin Growth Ventures ("AGV") is a private real estate investment company based in Austin, Texas. The company's focus is on real estate transactions in growth corridors around Austin.

It acquires, develops and manages properties with both internal equity and outside investors, with the goal of significant appreciation alongside quarterly dividend payments.

Strategy

AGV's mission is to provide profits to investors and owners of real estate through the competent formation of the package of assets and the use of market dynamics. The company's specificity consists in providing access to real estate market opportunities to all investors.

At the same time, the company maintains a high level of profitability, as in global investment holdings, and minimizes risks.

Principles

AGV activity is based on the key need of both clients and partners and founders of the investment company for high profitability.

The more comfortable and beneficial relations will be built with investors, the more and faster the business will develop. Exactly win-win for today is the key principle in the company's work.

SERVICES. Direct Investment and 3rd Party Advisory

Real Estate Investment

You can become a limited partner in a future new project. AGV is continuously acquiring assets across the Austin area for repositioning and development. Investors receive a fixed dividend coupon and equity participation in every deal.

As part of the investment, you will receive regular quarterly reports on the company's success with a clear calculation of your returns. Typically AGV targets full repayment of the original investment between 12-24 months.

Advisory Services

You can partner with AGV to underwrite and close real estate and development opportunities:

  • Site Feasibility Analysis
  • Leasing and Sales Advisory
  • Tax Protests
  • Acquisition, Construction and Permanent Debt Placement and Structuring
  • Distressed debt and foreclosure work
  • Auction Advisory
  • Construction design, budgeting and marketing analysis

Why Austin Growth Ventures?

Why

Austin Growth Ventures?

Competent Management

At the head of the investment company are experts with more than 10 years of experience in finance and real estate. This allows us to select only the best properties for investing capital and receive maximum benefits on the predictable dynamics of the market.

The best partnership at all levels

AGV carefully selects contractors and financial partners to work on each property. In addition, the company's experts are experienced in lobbying investors at all levels of the state to ensure only the best results for clients.

Transparent terms of cooperation

As part of co-investment, each partner receives favorable, clearly spelled out terms of transactions, which are strictly enforced. AGV publishes detailed public quarterly reports on all of the company's investments and deals

Safe investing

AGV experts have built processes so that investors are confident in the assets of their investments. This is a clearly calculated level of risk, return, and market volatility. Thanks to this, every investor can be calm about their capital and freely spend their leisure time instead of controlling the course of the company's affairs.
